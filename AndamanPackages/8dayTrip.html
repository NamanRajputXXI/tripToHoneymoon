8 Days Trip in Andaman - Details

Arrival at the magical Andamans and land at the Port Blair Airport in the morning. Our representative shall provide you a hearty welcome and arrange for your transfer to the hotel of your choice. After lunch and brief relaxation, we shall guide you to proceed to Chidiya Tapu that is located 25 kms away from Port Blair. The main attraction of Chidiya Tapu, placed in the southernmost tip of South Andamans, is the myriad variety of beautiful birds. Chidiya Tapu harbours more than 46 varieties of birds that includes drongos, parrots, scarlet minivet, emerald dove, long-tailed and red-breasted parakeets, white-bellied sea eagles and grey-fronted and imperial green pigeons to name a few. For this fact, Chidiya Tapu is also known as the Bird Island of Andaman and Nicobar Islands. After a jaunty excursion of Chidiya Tapu, return to the hotel at Port Blair for an overnight stay.

This day, after breakfast, you shall proceed to the Phoenix Bay Harbour to catch a ferry to Neil Island. On arrival, transfer to the hotel of your choice. Day at leisure to explore as you choose to, for example, the various exotic beaches at Neil island such as Laxmanpur Beach, Sitapur Beach, Bharatpur Beach and the Neil Island Jetty followed by an overnight stay at Neil Island. You might also choose to walk to the Howrah bridge to view the unnatural rock formation and maybe to the Neil Kendra Village for an out-of-the-box experience.

Neil Island has a lot of gorgeousness to offer to its guests – the clear, blue waters filled with colourful corals and even more colourful fishes and the ever mesmerizing sunrises and sunsets. After the morning breakfast, you shall be given a guided trip to Bharatpur Beach, Rock Bridge and Laxmanpur Beach. In the Afternoon, our representative shall pick you up and drop at the ferry jetty to travel from Neil Island to Havelock. The journey by ferry is of 2.5 to Havelock. Check-in at a hotel in Havelock for overnight stay.

This day is earmarked for an adventurous trip to Elephant Beach also known as the Beach No. 3. Snorkelling out of Elephant Beach is an ultimate experience. The shallow snorkelling waters can be reached by boat in 40 mins and the clam clear waters are worth a look for the colourful marine life underwater. The beach is also ideal for relaxing and sunbathing and also for spectacular photography shoot among the white sands against the dark remnants of fallen tree trunks.

An exciting day trip to Radhanagar Beach starts immediately after breakfast. Take in the exquisite beauty of the beach deemed as the best beach of Asia in a survey. Pick up shall be arranged from the hotel to the beach. Rest of the day is at leisure idling on the beach. Later in the day, set sail for Port Blair in a ferry and time the return in such a way to enjoy the crimson sun set across the unending ocean waters. Overnight stay at the hotel of your choice at Port Blair.

After breakfast, a full-day trip starts with the first stop at Coral Island also known as North Bay. Here, look out for the famous coral reefs along with exotic varieties of a plethora of beautiful ornamental fish and other sea creatures. Snorkel in the warm blue waters and enjoy the mysterious and enigmatic charm of underwater world. It is advisable to carry lunch packs, drinking water and a proper change of clothes as the water sports and adventure activities might leave you drenched. Next stop after North Bay, is the Ross Island, famous for its British-related heritage. Nicknamed as the Paris of the East, Ross Island is still replete with colonial legacy in terms of ruined dwellings, war bunkers, machinery and even churches. The palm tree lined island offers a restful and peaceful time with deer, peacock monkeys and birds. In the evening, we arrive at the third attraction of the day — Corbyn’s Cove Beach which is 7 kms from the heart of the city of Port Blair. This beach is ideal for sipping coconut, swimming, sun-bathing and relaxing. Overnight stay will be at the Port Blair hotel.

This day is reserved for a lovely and informative full-day city tour of historical Port Blair. Chatham Saw Mill that was one of the oldest and largest in Asia and now not in operation, Forest Museum, Mini Zoo, Fisheries, Marine Aquarium, Museum and Water Sports Complex are the major attractions. Cellular Jail with its captivating saga accompanied by the enthralling Sound and Light Show is the last but not the least attraction of the day. Watch the heroic saga of the national freedom struggle revived with pathos and sentiments. Overnight stay at the hotel at Port Blair.

Fly back home from the Port Blair airport with memories unlimited of a remarkable holiday.
