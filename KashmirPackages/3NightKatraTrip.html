3 Night Trip in Katra - Details

Pick up from Jammu Airport/Railway Station and transfer to Katra (50 Kms/1.5 Hours). Spend the rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Katra.

Early morning after breakfast, proceed for Mata Vaishno Devi Bhawan, one of the most popular pilgrimages in India. The yatra involves a trek of nearly 12 Kms from the base camp at Katra known as Ban Ganga. If time permits, visit Bhairo Temple and return to Katra in the evening. Overnight stay at Katra.

Early morning after breakfast, proceed for Shiv Khori, the Holy Cave abode of Lord Shiva. The cave is naturally formed and measures nearly half a kilometer. If time permits, en route, visit Siar Baba waterfalls, Baba Dhansal & Nau Devis. Overnight stay at Katra.

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to Jammu to drop at Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey with sweet memories of the tour.
