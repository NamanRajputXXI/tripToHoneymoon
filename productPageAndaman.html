About Andaman:

Embrace the tropical paradise of the Andaman Islands, surrounded by crystal-clear turquoise waters and pristine sandy beaches. Your stay in Andaman promises not only relaxation but also a thrilling experience with a variety of water adventures.

The accommodation provides a perfect blend of comfort and beachfront luxury, offering mesmerizing views of the azure sea. Immerse yourself in the tranquil atmosphere and explore the vibrant marine life and coral reefs.

Don't miss the opportunity to enjoy the island breeze, and witness spectacular sunsets that paint the sky in a myriad of colors.

Activities in Andaman:

The Andaman Islands offer a range of activities for beach lovers and adventure seekers. Dive into the vibrant underwater world through snorkeling and scuba diving adventures. Explore the white sandy beaches, take a boat tour to discover hidden coves, and indulge in water sports like kayaking and jet-skiing.

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage by visiting historic sites and museums. Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the beaches, and don't forget to savor the local seafood delicacies.

About Meals:

While you have the option to bring your own snacks and beverages, we highly recommend trying the local seafood delicacies. Experience the flavors of Andaman's culinary delights, and our staff can assist in arranging meals from nearby eateries for an authentic taste of the islands.

How to Reach Andaman:

The Andaman Islands are accessible by air and sea. Enjoy a scenic flight or cruise to this tropical paradise. Here's how you can reach the Andaman Islands:

  • Nearest Airport: Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair
  • Nearest Sea Port: Port Blair Harbor

Itinerary

Day 1
4.6
Your Chosen Hotel in Port Blair
4
Arrival at historical Port Blair
Morning arrival at Port Blair and a warm welcome by our tour guide followed by transfer to your chosen hotel. Spend a relaxing afternoon. In the evening, proceed for a guided tour to enjoy the mesmerizing Light and Sound Show at the Cellular Jail complex. Listen to the heroic tale of the Indian freedom struggle and get inspired. Overnight stay at the hotel in Port Blair.
Day 2
4.8
Your Chosen Hotel in Havelock
4.5
Transfer from Port Blair to Havelock / Excursion to Radhanagar Beach
Pick up from hotel by a designated tour guide by car at 7.30 a.m. and drop at the jetty for the morning ferry scheduled at 8.30 a.m. from Port Blair to leave for Havelock. The ferry ride is 2.5 hours. On arrival, check-in at the hotel of your choice in Havelock. After a brief rest and time to get fresh, cab is arranged for pickup and drop till Beach No 7 also known as Radhanagar beach rated as the best beach in Asia. Rest of the day is at leisure at Radhanagar Beach. Overnight stay at Havelock Island.
Day 3
4.7
Your Chosen Hotel in Neil Island
4.2
Excursion to Elephant beach inclusive of one snorkelling session and transfer from Havelock to Neil Island
This day marks a day trip to Elephant Beach or Beach No.3. This beach offers the most exotic snorkelling experience and can be reached by a 40 min water trip by boat. The calm blue shallow waters with a radiant show of colourful sea animals and corals make this spot ideal for snorkelers. The scenic beach also provides a relaxing beach lazing and sun bathing experience. The uprooted dry tree stumps on the white sands offers an interesting location for photo shoot. The coral reef starts within a range of one meter from the shore. The reef is composed of hard coral and contains a myriad range of marine life. The package includes the charges for the snorkelling equipment apart from the pick and drop. In the afternoon, drop at the Phoenix Bay Harbour of Havelock for onward journey to Neil Island via ferry. Check- in and overnight stay at Neil Island.
Day 4
4.5
Your Chosen Hotel in Port Blair
4.9
Day sojourn of Neil Island and transfer from Neil Island to Port Blair
After an early sumptuous breakfast, our tour representative shall guide you through a comfortable visit to the local attractions such as the Bharatpur Beach, Rock Bridge and Laxmanpur Beach. In the afternoon, after the sightseeing, transfer to Port Blair via ferry. Check-in and overnight stay at Port Blair hotel.
