About Kerala:

Embraced by the Western Ghats and lapped by the Arabian Sea, Kerala is a land of diverse landscapes and vibrant culture. Your stay in Kerala promises not only stunning views but also an immersion in its rich heritage and natural splendor.

The accommodation offers a blend of comfort and tradition, with some rooms providing mesmerizing views of the backwaters. Immerse yourself in the tranquil atmosphere and explore the local culture and cuisine.

Make sure to seize the moments on private houseboat cruises, drifting along the serene backwaters and enjoying the unique charm of Kerala's landscapes.

The property comes alive at night, adorned with subtle lights that add to the enchantment of the surroundings.

Activities in Kerala:

Kerala offers a variety of activities for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. Glide through the backwaters on a houseboat, surrounded by the verdant hills. Visit the tea estates and spice plantations for a sensory journey. Explore the lush landscapes through trekking expeditions and camp under the starry skies. Immerse yourself in traditional music and dance performances that showcase Kerala's artistic legacy.

Don't miss the chance to explore the local markets, offering an array of handcrafted items such as traditional clothing, spices, and intricate handicrafts. Engage with local artisans to appreciate the skill behind these treasures.

About Meals:

While you are welcome to bring your own food and beverages, we recommend indulging in the local Kerala cuisine. Experience the flavors of traditional dishes and enjoy the unique culinary delights that Kerala has to offer. The property manager can assist in arranging meals from nearby restaurants, providing you with a taste of Kerala's hospitality.

How to Reach Kerala:

Kerala is accessible by road, rail, and air. The picturesque journey to the state is an integral part of the experience. Here's how you can reach Kerala:

  • Nearest Bus Stand: Trivandrum Central Bus Station (Approx. 200 km)
  • Nearest Railway Station: Ernakulam Junction (Approx. 150 km)
  • Nearest Airport: Trivandrum International Airport (Approx. 230 km)
